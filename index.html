League Rules

League format, GHIN requirements, playoff setup, and tiebreaker order.

Format

Weekly 2-man best ball match play. Each matchup is played over 9 holes.

GHIN Handicaps

GHIN handicaps are mandatory. GHINs will be used for league handicapping throughout the season, so all players are expected to keep their GHIN updated and post scores after each round.

Playoff Format

All 8 teams make the playoffs. The opening round is seeded bracket-style with 1 vs. 8, 2 vs. 7, 3 vs. 6, and 4 vs. 5.

After each playoff round, teams are reseeded so the highest remaining seed plays the lowest remaining seed.

If teams are tied with the same record in the standings, the tiebreaker is total holes won for the season.

Tiebreakers

Playoff holes will no longer be used. For both the regular season and playoffs, ties will be broken using the following structure:

  1. Combined net total
  2. Net scores on the hardest holes, essentially skins
  3. Most net birdies in the round
  4. Most gross pars
